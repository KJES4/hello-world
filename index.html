Hello, I’m Kathryn.

I'm originally from Clearwater, Florida.

Background

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I've spent the last four years of my life living and working in China where I owned and operated an English training facility. I’m excited to bring my love of design to the process of building fantastic looking websites. My favorite part of web design is helping purpose-driven business owners to find their unique voice and then finding ways to represent that unique voice in every element of their online presence.

I am a life-long learner who's always interested in expanding my skills. I have a number of interests ranging from technology to literature to astronomy. I take pride in working to better myself so that I can contribute more to society.
